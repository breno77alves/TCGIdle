# Skill: design-idle-economy

## Purpose
Design expedition pacing, reward cadence, currency sinks, offline caps, and long-term collection flow.

## Output Format
1. target player experience
2. resource map: sources and sinks
3. proposed numbers
4. pacing assumptions
5. abuse/failure risks
6. tuning levers

## Coverage
- expedition duration
- drop count and quality bands
- essence generation
- reroll costs
- streak rewards
- zone unlock pacing
- offline progression cap

## Rules
- connect all numbers to a player-facing feeling
- avoid dead resources
- avoid infinite runaway reward loops
